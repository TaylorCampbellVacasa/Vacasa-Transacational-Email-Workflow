Additional info

Changing or canceling your reservation

No more searching your inbox or snapping screenshots--get entry codes in offline mode, then connect to WiFi instantly when you walk in the door.

Early check-in and late check-out

We'll email you the morning of your arrival if early check-in is available, and two to three days before your departure if late check-out is available.

Accessing your Vacasa home

We'll send you everything you need—including driving directions, entry codes, and internet access information—the week before your trip.

We may send you text messages as part of this transaction, before check-in and shortly after check-out. These messages could include a link to download and manage your reservation in our mobile app and/or a link to leave a review of your stay.
